[Votingo - Biometric Authenticated Blockchain Voting System]

"This project develops a secure digital voting system by integrating face recognition with a permissioned blockchain to eliminate duplicate voting, prevent manipulation, and enable voting even in low-connectivity rural environments."

Problem Statement

Traditional voting systems face impersonation, ballot tampering, lack of transparency, and operational challenges in rural areas. A tamper-proof, identity-verifiable, and offline-capable voting solution is required to ensure fair elections.

Literature Review / Market Research

Existing EVMs and online voting platforms provide efficiency but lack end-to-end auditability and rural adaptability. Public blockchain-based solutions are impractical due to cost, latency, and infrastructure needs.

Research Gap / Innovation

This system uniquely combines biometric authentication with a permissioned blockchain using RAFT consensus to ensure secure, traceable, and offline-synchronizable voting.

System Methodology

Dataset / Input

Live facial capture, stored voter embeddings, hashed identity records, and locally cached vote transactions used for blockchain submission.

Model / Architecture

Face verification → Unique voting token → Blockchain transaction → Consensus validation → Immutable storage → Offline sync capability.
